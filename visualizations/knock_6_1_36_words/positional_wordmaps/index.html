Understanding Positional Embeddings

These visualizations show the learned positional embeddings from your NanoGPT model:

  • Position Numbers: Each wordmap shows position labels ([0], [1], [2], etc.) indicating sequence positions
  • Size indicates strength: Larger position numbers have stronger influence on that dimension
  • Color indicates sign: Black for positive values, red for negative values
  • Learned patterns: These aren't simple counting patterns - they're complex learned representations

Key insight: Unlike simple positional encoding, these embeddings learned which positions are most important for each semantic dimension during training.
