Understanding Q/K/V Transformations

These visualizations show how token embeddings are transformed into attention spaces:

  • Query (Q): How words "ask" - what information they seek from other positions
  • Key (K): How words "advertise" - what information they offer to be attended to
  • Value (V): How words "contribute" - what information they provide when attended to

Key insight: Each Q/K/V dimension represents a different "type" of attention relationship learned during training.
